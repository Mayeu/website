What is Web-Of-Trust

The Web of Trust is a buzzword for a new model of decentralized self-sovereign identity. Itâ€™s a phrase that dates back almost twenty-five years, the classic definition derives from PGP. But some use it as a term to include self-sovereign identity authentication & verification, certificate validation, and reputation assessment, while the vibrant blockchain community is also drawing new attention to the concept we aim to reboot it.
