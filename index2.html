Rebooting the Web of Trust



Rebooting the Web of Trust is a cooperative of tech visionaries experienced with security, privacy, and identity. Its goal is to imagine and implement the future of decentralized trust on the internet. The first 40 members of the group came together at a design workshop held in San Francisco, California on November 3rd and November 4th, 2015. The purpose of that initial workshop was to produce 3-5 white papers or design specs, the first of which appear below.

Moving forward, Rebooting the Web of Trust's major goal is to create a notable product for release during July 2016, to commemorate the 25th anniversary of PGP, the software that created the first Web of Trust. To accomplish this goal, we are planning more design workshops, hackathons, or other get-togethers in the first half of 2016.

If you'd like to be involved in the future of the Web of Trust or would like to help sponsor these events, email ChristopherA@LifeWithAlacrity.com.
